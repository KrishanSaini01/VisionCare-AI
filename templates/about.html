About VisionCare AI

Intelligent Medical Image Analysis Platform powered by Deep Learning and Generative AI.

Our Mission

VisionCare AI helps healthcare professionals and patients by analyzing medical images using Deep Learning. The platform predicts diseases from medical images and provides AI-generated explanations using Google's Gemini AI.

The goal is to improve early disease detection while making medical reports easier to understand.

Key Features

CNN Prediction

Detect diseases from Chest X-rays using a trained CNN model.

Gemini AI

Get medical explanations, precautions and answers through AI.

Medical Reports

Generate professional medical reports with confidence scores.
